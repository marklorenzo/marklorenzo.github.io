Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Timmy Works Consulting provide?
We specialize in Claris FileMaker Development, Claris Connect Integration, FileMaker for Web and Mobile, and custom software solutions tailored to your business needs.
What is FileMaker and why should I use it?
FileMaker is a powerful low-code platform from Claris that allows businesses to create custom apps for managing data, workflows, and business processes. It’s flexible, scalable, and can be deployed on desktop, web, and mobile.
Do you provide ongoing support after development?
Yes, we offer maintenance and support packages to ensure your systems remain efficient, secure, and up-to-date.
Can you integrate FileMaker with other tools?
Absolutely. Using Claris Connect and APIs, we can integrate FileMaker with many third-party applications like CRMs, accounting software, cloud storage, and more.
How long does it take to build a FileMaker solution?
The timeline depends on the complexity of your requirements. Simple systems can take a few weeks, while larger enterprise-level projects may take several months. We’ll provide a project plan during the consultation stage.
Do you work with businesses outside the Philippines?
Yes! We work with international clients and provide remote support, training, and development services globally.
