About Timmy Works Consulting

Empowering Your Business Needs with Customer FileMaker Solutions

Who We Are

Timmy Works specializes in custom FileMaker solutions designed to streamline your workflow and boost productivity. With years of hands-on experience, we help businesses of all sizes solve complex data and process challenges using the power of FileMaker. From planning to deployment and support, we partner closely with our clients to deliver smart, scalable, and user-friendly systems.

About the Founder

Mark Lorenzo is a Certified Claris Filemaker Pro Developer, a Certified Claris Connect Developer, a Database Developer, and a God-fearing Christian with 10+ years experience in the software industry and 4+ years as Filemaker Pro Developer.


He's been working in the software industry for more than 10 years with experiences in developing application such native Android Application, native iOS Applications, Web Applications using Ruby on Rails, Java, .NET Core and PHP. But more recently, his latest experience is on Database Development and FileMaker Applications.


With his experiences, he was able to learn the entire process of Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC), including planning, requirements gathering, Moqups, tasks estimation, budget estimation, development, testing, deployment and UAT. Knowledgeable to waterfall and iterative processes but his expertise is Agile development since it's the most beneficial to clients. Mark is also proficient in all aspects development in FileMaker, including architecture design, UI design, complex database structure, scripting, security, web publishing using Webdirect, FileMaker cloud, and third-party API integrations.


Mark has the goal to help clients automate their manual tasks by producing an application that is efficient but cost friendly. And also learn from their business for his overall growth.
